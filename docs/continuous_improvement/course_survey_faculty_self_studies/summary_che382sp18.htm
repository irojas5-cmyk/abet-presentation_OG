[Hours per week for this course: 4-6] It would be helpful if there was a little more guidance on the equipment being used in the lab. I thought that this was very helpful and ensured that the groups were able to complete the experiment. Another recommendation I would give is to require that the group submit their template for data collection in their pre-lab. The TA's could check the excel data sheet to make sure you have the appropriate measurements for data collection. The final recommendation is to officially switch the due dates of the labs to Mondays at midnight. This allows groups more time to work on final calculations/editing after classes on Mondays.
